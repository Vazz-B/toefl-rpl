## Findings
- **Access State**: Publicly accessible via Figma Make. No login required for viewing.
- **Design Title**: Sistem Pelayanan TOEFL Online UTM.
- **Color Palette**: Deep Blue (Primary), Orange/Yellow (CTA), White/Light Gray (Backgrounds).
- **Navigation**: Beranda, Layanan, Jadwal, Cara Daftar, FAQ.
- **Key Sections**:
    - **Hero**: Catchy headline, service description, and "Daftar TOEFL Sekarang" button.
    - **Stats**: 5000+ Students, 98% pass rate, 450+ sessions/year.
    - **Layanan/Features**: 6 cards covering Online Reg, Flexible Schedule, Computer-based testing, Digital Certs, Auto Notifications, and 24/7 Support.
    - **Cara Daftar**: 5-step process (Account creation -> Schedule -> Payment -> Test -> Download Cert).
    - **Jadwal Tes**: Daily slots with status (Available, Almost Full, Full) and location details.
- **Sidebar Suggestions**: Integrated login (NIM), Payment Gateway (Midtrans/DOKU), Admin Dashboard.
